ABOUT THE CAFE

My heart has a special spot for the Book Club Café. It's a warm, comfortable place where I can get out of the stresses of daily life and lose everything to the book world. This café stands out for its beautiful mix of literary culture, amazing food, and friendly service.

As soon as I walk in, a cheerful service person welcomes me and asks what my drink of choice is. The furnishings are attractive and inviting, with bookcases covering the walls, soft sofas, and lots of windows. It's the kind of place that tempts you to stay awhile, drift off, and lose yourself in a good book.

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 8pm.

Address: 197 E 3rd St, New York, NY 10009
